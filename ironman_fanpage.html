About Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is the alter ego of one of the most iconic superheroes in the Marvel Universe. With his self-built powered suit of armor, Tony Stark has fought villains and saved the world countless times, becoming a founding member of the Avengers. Iron Man is not only known for his heroics but also for his wit, intelligence, and complex personality.
